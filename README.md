## 👋 About Me

I'm a Business Analytics student who enjoys solving business problems with data.

Most of my work starts from a simple question:
**"What is happening, why is it happening, and what should we do next?"**

From there, I work through the full analytics process: cleaning messy data, exploring patterns, building dashboards or models, and translating the results into clear business recommendations.

My interests are mainly in:

* 📊 **Business Intelligence & KPI Dashboards**
* 🧠 **Machine Learning for Business Problems**
* 🏦 **Banking, Credit Risk & Customer Analytics**
* 🛒 **Funnel, Sales & Product Performance Analysis**
* 🤖 **Generative AI & Workflow Automation**

I like projects that connect technical work with real business decisions — not just building models, but making the results understandable and useful for stakeholders.
